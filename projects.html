Stealth & Puzzle Adventure

Escape The Teddys

Escape The Teddys is a stealth-driven puzzle adventure built around a unique multi-character control system.

Players sequentially control six toy bears, each with distinct abilities. The core gameplay revolves around chaining these abilities together in an interconnected system to solve puzzles.

To succeed, players must:

  • ▹ Strategically switch between characters
  • ▹ Use abilities in the correct sequence
  • ▹ Create distractions and avoid detection
Vision

To evolve the prototype into a polished, scalable puzzle experience with strong replayability and a distinctive gameplay identity.
